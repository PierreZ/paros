paros · single-decree Paxos, deterministic, in your browser tab

A client asks a 3-acceptor cluster to agree on one value. A proposer runs Phase 1 (Prepare → Promise) to claim a ballot, then Phase 2 (Accept → Accepted) to get a value chosen by a majority. The whole simulation, including moonpool's real transport stack and a chaotic network, runs as wasm. Same seed ⇒ same run. No two acceptors ever choose different values.
